School Invaders is a derivative of Space Invaders. The 
objective of this game is to survive as long as 
possible and get the highest Score. You do this by 
using the left/right arrow keys to move left and right.
You will use the space bar to shoot. You may want to 
pick your shots carefully as there is a cooldown. Be 
careful as the enemies will shoot back but don't worry 
you initially get some barriers(desks) to hide behind.
Good luck and most importantly enjoy.
